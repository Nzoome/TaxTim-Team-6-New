   - Click individual rows for specific transactions
   - Check the summary cards for quick overview
   - Note the date range to verify completeness

   - Read error messages carefully (they reference line numbers)
   - Fix one error at a time
   - Re-upload after fixing
   - Use the tips section for guidance

   - The loading spinner shows processing is active
   - Don't refresh during upload
